BEL SOIL gives your team a structured backend for running scenario-based readiness analysis across workplace, financial, and environmental domains. Every output is explicitly scoped as decision support — no autonomous determinations, no certifications, no automatic model changes. Human review is always required before acting on any SOIL output.

## What BEL SOIL Does

BEL SOIL runs governed scenario analysis against a configurable set of sector packs. You describe conditions and events in your organization; SOIL assembles a structured output package that your reviewers can act on.

## Get Started in Three Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a SOIL Session">
    Call `POST /createSoilSession` with your `tenantId` and `organizationId` to establish a session context. You'll receive a `soilSessionId` to use in all subsequent requests.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Run a Scenario">
    Call `POST /createScenarioRun` with your session ID, the target sector pack, and the conditions and events you want to model. SOIL validates the input and returns a structured scenario run object.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the Output">
    Retrieve results with `GET /getScenarioRun`. All outputs carry `decisionSupportOnly: true` and `humanReviewRequired: true`. Route them to your review workflow before taking any action.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  BEL SOIL outputs are always marked `READY_FOR_REVIEW`. Final approval and any downstream action remain with your authorized reviewers — never with the system itself.
</Note>
